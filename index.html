Human Computer Interaction Programming

Smoking Trend is a web-based application that provides a hands on experience with the support of visualizations from the data. The goal is to find a trend between high school teens in San Diego and their ethnicity and income by visualizing it into four different graphs. By knowing the percentage of high school teens that smoke in each ethnicity, Smoking Trends compares it with areas of low and high income and the different ethnicities that occupy the area.
